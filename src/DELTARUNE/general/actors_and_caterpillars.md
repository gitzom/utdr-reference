
# Actors and Caterpillars
Actors (obj_actor) in Deltarune represent characters in a cutscene.

Caterpillars (obj_caterpillarchara) in Deltarune are characters who follow you in the overworld. Caterpillars are created whenever you have a party member and are entering a room in the overworld.

# obj_caterpillarchara

`obj_caterpillarchata attributes` | Value
--- | ---
`.name' | Name of the character 



# scr_maincharacters_actors()
A script that transforms the current caterpillars loaded in the room into actors, usually in preparation for a cutscene.
